Privacy Policy Last Updated: 9/19/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how OG's Company. “we,” “our,” or “us” collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you create an account or use our services (Sign Up Page).

When you register for an account, we collect the personal information you provide such as name, email address, phone number, and password. We may also collect technical information about your device, browser, and usage patterns to improve our Services.

Communicate with you about your account or updates

We may share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate the Services such as payment processors, hosting providers, or email systems. These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at ContactOG@gmail.com .

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through the platform.

By creating an account, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
